The Treatment

How Laser Pigmentation
Treatment Works

1

Targets Melanin Directly

Laser treatment is a highly effective, clinically proven method for reversing visible sun damage. It works by targeting melanin to clear spots, sealing broken blood vessels, and stimulating collagen production — dramatically improving skin texture with results often lasting for years.

2

Selective Laser Technology

We use pigment and sunspot-specific selective lasers to safely target excess melanin in the skin, breaking up brown spots and age spots. The laser heats the inner layers of the skin without damaging the surface.

3

Minimal Recovery, Lasting Results

Treatments require very little recovery time, though typically a number of sessions are needed to see noticeable results. The required number of sessions is determined at your free no-obligation consultation, based on your skin type and the severity of the damage.

4

Temporary, Mild Side Effects Only

Minor temporary side effects like redness and swelling are common and usually subside within a few days to a week. The effectiveness of treatment largely depends on your skin type and the severity of the damage.
